ASTAR Lab: Autonomous Systems TRust & Assurance

Our mission is to advance the security, resilience, and trustworthiness of AI-powered autonomous systems. We focus on safeguarding UAVs, autonomous vehicles, and smart transportation networks against cyber and physical threats through cutting-edge research in trustworthy AI, secure localization, multi-modal sensing, and resilient cyber-physical infrastructures.
